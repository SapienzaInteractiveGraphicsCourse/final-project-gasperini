Welcome!

This is the 3D Tic-Tac-Toe game, but don't assume you already know everything, since there are surprises! Please read these brief instructions.

The game is divided into two steps.
In the first one the players will have to drive a small car, trying to catch as many coins as possible, falling from the sky. Each coin represents a move useful for the next step of the game: the gained moves will be added to the corresponding player.
You can move the car only forward, pressing the "Space" Key, and you can steer, pressing "A/D" keys.

In the second step the players will play the classic game, but on a huge cube containing six boards. Players have to be smart since moves are limited.

Have fun!
