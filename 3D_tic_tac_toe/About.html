Welcome!

This is the 3D Tic-Tac-Toe game, but don't suppose you already know everything, since there are surprises that now I will spoiler you.

The game is divided into two steps.
The first one is called "Gain Moves", since the players will have to drive a small car, trying to catch as many coins as possible, falling from the sky. Each coin represents a move useful for the next step of the game: the gained moves will be added to the corresponding player.

In the second step the players will play the classic tic-tac-toe game, but on a huge cube containing six boards. Players have to be smart since moves are limited! The game has been devised and implemented by me. Have fun!
